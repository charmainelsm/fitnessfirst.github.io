Recommended Classes

AERIAL FLOW YOGA

Elevate your practice with Aerial Yoga.
Taking place mid-air as you flow from pose to pose on a Yoga Swing, this unique prop takes the strain off your body letting your practice be longer and more comfortable.
There is strength to be gain from Aerial Flow Yoga. In order to support your back, your core will work hard. Aerial Flow Yoga has its restorative and therapeutic benefits by relieving tightness in neck, shoulder and lower back.

HIGH INTENSITY INTERVAL (HIIT) X STRENGTH

Push through bursts of explosive exercises and keeping that heart rate high.
Taking short breaks in between sets and exercises to recover and repeat, this class helps to work on functional body strength through the use of dumbbells, kettlebells and many more.
This HIITXSTRENGTH class is a challenge for you to take on.
